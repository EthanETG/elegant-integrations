About Us

Control your home from a single screen.

Elegant Integrations is the leading home technology integration company serving the Southeast Florida area. With over 30 years of experience implementing surround sound, automation, lighting control, and security systems in coastal-style homes we have perfected the art of integrating the technology our clients want while preserving the carefully designed and crafted look of their homes.

The cornerstone of our philosophy is that people don't work for technology, technology works for people. With this in mind we work hand-in-hand with our clients to create a home automation experience that is effective and simple to use.

Experience has also taught us that ease of use and reliability are key to the enjoyment of any home-based technology. For this reason we use only trusted brands with proven track records. We help you personalize your home with the home automation system of your dreams - all within your budget. Our passion is to deliver an exceptional automation experience for our customers.

Finally, providing prompt and efficient service has allowed us to build our business into what it is today. We pride ourselves on continuously surpassing our clients expectations, helping them get the most enjoyment from their investment. Let us design and implement a system that will enhance the enjoyment you get from your time spent in your home.

We integrate everything from lighting control, music, home theater, climate control, alarm, security cameras, appliances—even iPads, iPhones and Android smartphones and tablets—a smart house by Elegant Integrations creates personalized experiences that enhance your life and provide added comfort, savings, convenience, and peace of mind.

Whether you want to start small by automating just your family room or theater or you want to go big with whole-home automation, we invite you to contact us to discuss the possibilities. No matter what the application, the experience is unforgettable and you'll wonder how you ever lived without home automation.
